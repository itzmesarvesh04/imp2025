About Us

Intro

Impact Computer Institute is a government-recognized computer training center with a mission to provide quality, affordable computer education. Our goal is to empower students with the skills needed to succeed in today‚Äôs digital world.

Our Journey

Established in January 1997 in Sector 22 of Koparkhairane with just four computers, Impact Computer Institute has grown steadily over the years. Today, we operate four active branches across Koparkhairane, Ghansoli, and Bonkode.

Our Growth and Values

Our success is built on the dedication of our passionate teaching staff and the trust of thousands of students and parents. Each student's achievement reflects our commitment to providing effective, industry-relevant education.
